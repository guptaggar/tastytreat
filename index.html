Geeta Aggarwal is an associate professor in PGDAV College. She graduated in Computer Science from Miranda House, University of Delhi in 1988 following which she completed her masters from Banasthali Vidyapeeth in 1990. She worked in Kulachi Hansraj Model School (run by DAV committee) for five years. Thereafter she joined as Systems Analyst in National Informatics Centre, Delhi for a short period following which she joined PGDAV College, University of Delhi in 1996. She has done Ph.D under Dr. Neelima Gupta, University of Delhi in the field of Bioinformatics. She has published research papers in international conferences.
